Our Story

Niyantri Studio was founded with a passion for storytelling through light, texture, and emotion. We blend documentary-style photography with refined portraiture to create images that feel intimate and real.

Meet the Photographer

The studio is led by a photographer who thrives on authentic connections and natural light. With a background in portrait and lifestyle, our approach centers on making clients feel comfortable and telling their story with patience and discretion.
